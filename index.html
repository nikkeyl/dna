About Nucleotides

Nucleotides are the basic building blocks of DNA and RNA:

  • A (Adenine)
  • T (Thymine) — DNA only
  • U (Uracil) — RNA only
  • C (Cytosine)
  • G (Guanine)

⚠️ Disclaimer: This visualization is an artistic and educational tool. It does not reflect realistic molecular biology or actual genetics.

Reference Guide

  • Enter characters A, T, C, G or paste a vertical list of sequences.
  • Max Stability algorithm simulates generations to find robust G-C clusters.
  • Iterate finds the strongest, weakest and mutated variants from a list.
  • Mutate Sequence modifies one random base.
